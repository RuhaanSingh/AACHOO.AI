AACHOO: Breathe easier with personalized allergy insights.

AACHOO is a comprehensive allergy tracking app that harnesses location data and environmental analytics to empower individuals with allergies to make informed decisions about their outdoor activities.

OUR PRODUCT

Empowering those with allergies: AACHOO understands the daily struggles of people with allergies. That's why we've created an app that helps you stay ahead of the curve. Our advanced AI learning model analyzes air conditions, pollen levels, and your location to provide personalized allergy forecasts.

  • Knowledge is power: No more guesswork when it comes to your allergies. With AACHOO, you receive tailored insights and recommendations, enabling you to make informed decisions and confidently enjoy the outdoors.
  • This innovative web application utilizes geospatial data and environmental sensors to provide a granular risk analysis of outdoor conditions across geographic locales. By tapping into atmospheric data streams and pollution monitors, the site can ascertain the current ambient toxicology and allergen landscape for a given set of coordinates. These external data inputs are then computed through a proprietary algorithm to yield multi-factorial safety scores, indexing the degree of peril posed by particulate matters, harmful gasses, pollen levels, and other exogenous threats present in the air.
